What is NourishSense?

NourishSense is a next-generation food and health experience designed to help people choose healthier meals before they decide. It combines prediction, behavior science, and personalization to make habit change simple and sustainable.

Key Features

Predictive Alerts

Detect risky moments before meals and send supportive nudges to prevent unhealthy choices.

Smart Nudges

Use personalized, context-aware recommendations instead of generic advice.

Lifestyle Adaptation

Adjust goals and suggestions dynamically based on sleep, stress, travel, and routine changes.

AI Coaching

Provide adaptive meal swaps, grocery planning, and habit coaching tailored to each user.

System Architecture

The app uses a mobile frontend, backend AI services, and integrations with health APIs and device data. A central recommendation engine powers real-time prediction and personalization.

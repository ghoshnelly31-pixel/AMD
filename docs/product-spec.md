# NourishSense Product Specification

## Vision
Help people make healthier food choices before they decide, using context-aware prediction, smart nudges, and adaptive personalization.

## App Name
**NourishSense**

## Value Proposition
- Predict unhealthy eating moments before they occur.
- Deliver timely, supportive nudges that respect user preferences.
- Adapt continuously to lifestyle changes, stress, sleep, and routine.

## Target Users
- Busy professionals with limited meal planning time.
- People managing weight or metabolic health.
- Individuals with dietary restrictions or food sensitivities.
- Health-conscious users seeking smarter habit support.

## Core Features
### Basic
- Personal profile, goals, and dietary preferences.
- Manual meal logging with nutrition summary.
- Daily progress dashboard and habit reminders.
- Simple food suggestions.

### Intermediate
- Predictive unhealthy eating alerts.
- Context-aware nudges based on time, location, and mood.
- Adaptive daily goals reflecting progress.
- Wearable and health-data integration.
- Mood, stress, and hunger tracking.

### Advanced
- Pre-emptive risk prediction for upcoming meals.
- AI-powered meal swaps, grocery suggestions, and recipes.
- Image-based meal recognition and nutrition estimation.
- Personalized coaching and dynamic habit adaptation.
- Social accountability, family plans, and expert programs.
